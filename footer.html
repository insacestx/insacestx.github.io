Text Messaging Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At ACES Insurance Services, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Text Messaging Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when you opt in to receive SMS/text messages from us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect your mobile phone number, your name (if provided), message content you send to us, and delivery/interaction data. We do not collect sensitive personal information through text messaging.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to send appointment reminders, policy updates, claims assistance, customer service responses, and important service notifications. We do not send unsolicited marketing messages.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use secure systems, encrypted platforms, and access controls. We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for marketing.

4. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions with us. Standard message and data rates may apply.

5. Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

6. Third-Party Messaging Providers

We may use trusted third-party platforms to deliver messages. These providers must protect your information and follow all applicable privacy laws.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to your data, ask for deletion, or update your contact information by contacting us at office@insaces.com or (254) 709‑6487.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Updates will be posted on our website.
