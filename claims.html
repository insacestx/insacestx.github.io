What To Do When You Need to File a Claim

Whether it’s auto, home, commercial, or life insurance — ACES is here to guide you.

1. Stay Safe

Make sure everyone involved is safe. Call emergency services if needed.

2. Document the Incident

Take photos, gather details, and write down what happened.

3. Contact Your Agent

Your ACES agent will walk you through the next steps and help file the claim.

4. File Your Claim

We’ll help you submit everything to the carrier and follow up on your behalf.

Information You Should Have Ready

Having these details prepared will help speed up your claim:

• Policy number
• Date and time of incident
• Photos or videos of damage
• Description of what happened
• Contact information for any third parties
• Police report number (if applicable)

Qué Hacer Cuando Necesite Presentar un Reclamo

Ya sea auto, hogar, comercial o vida — ACES está aquí para guiarle.
