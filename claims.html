How Claims Work

📞

1. Report the Claim

Contact your carrier or your ACES agent to begin the process.

📸

2. Gather Documentation

Photos, receipts, and details help speed up your claim.

🧑‍💼

3. Adjuster Assigned

A licensed adjuster will review your loss and guide next steps.

✔️

4. Claim Resolution

Once approved, your claim will be settled according to your policy.
