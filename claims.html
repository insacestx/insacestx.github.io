Before You File a Claim

Review the steps below to ensure a smooth claims experience.

📸

Take Photos

Document all damage or incidents clearly.

📝

Write Down Details

Include dates, times, and what happened.

👥

Gather Information

Collect names, contact info, and any third-party details.

What to Have Ready

  • Policy number
  • Date and time of incident
  • Photos or videos of damage
  • Description of what happened
  • Third-party information
