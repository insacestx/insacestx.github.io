Commercial Property

Protect your building, equipment, and business assets.

Overview

Commercial Property insurance protects your building, equipment, inventory, and physical assets from losses caused by fire, theft, storms, vandalism, and more.

Coverage Highlights

  • Building coverage for owned commercial structures.
  • Protection for equipment, tools, and inventory.
  • Coverage for fire, theft, storms, and vandalism.
  • Business interruption coverage available.

Why Choose ACES?

  • We help you determine accurate property values and coverage needs.
  • Access to multiple commercial carriers for competitive pricing.
  • Local, bilingual agents who understand Texas business risks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Commercial Property cover floods?

Flooding is typically excluded. We can help you obtain separate flood coverage if needed.

Does this cover my tools and equipment?

Yes, but limits vary. For mobile equipment, Inland Marine coverage may be recommended.
