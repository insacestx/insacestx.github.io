Workers Compensation

Protect your employees — and your business — from workplace injuries.

Overview

Workers Compensation insurance provides medical benefits and wage replacement for employees who are injured on the job. It also protects your business from costly lawsuits related to workplace injuries.

Coverage Highlights

  • Medical expenses for work-related injuries.
  • Wage replacement for injured employees.
  • Protection from employee injury lawsuits.
  • Coverage for rehabilitation and recovery services.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Workers Compensation required in Texas?

Texas does not require all employers to carry Workers Comp, but many industries, contracts, and clients do require it.

Does Workers Comp cover part-time employees?

Yes. Workers Compensation typically covers full-time, part-time, and seasonal employees.
