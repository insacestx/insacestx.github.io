Renters Insurance

Affordable protection for your belongings in a rented home or apartment.

Overview

Renters insurance helps cover your personal property and liability, even if you don’t own the building.

Coverage Highlights

  • Coverage for personal belongings due to covered losses.
  • Liability protection if someone is injured in your rental.
  • Additional living expenses if you’re temporarily displaced.

Why Choose ACES?

  • Simple, budget-friendly options for renters.
  • Local agents who can explain coverage in plain language.
  • Bilingual support for you and your family.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does my landlord’s insurance cover my belongings?

No. A landlord’s policy typically covers the building, not your personal property.

Is renters insurance expensive?

Renters insurance is often very affordable and can provide valuable protection.
