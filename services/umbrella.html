Umbrella Insurance

Extra liability protection for life’s unexpected moments.

Overview

Umbrella insurance provides an extra layer of liability protection beyond your auto, home, or renters policies—helping safeguard your assets and future.

Coverage Highlights

  • Additional liability coverage beyond standard policy limits.
  • Protection for major accidents, lawsuits, and unexpected claims.
  • Coverage that extends across multiple policies.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need umbrella insurance?

If you want extra protection for your assets or have higher liability risks, umbrella coverage is a smart choice.

Does umbrella insurance cover my family?

Yes. Most umbrella policies extend coverage to household members.
