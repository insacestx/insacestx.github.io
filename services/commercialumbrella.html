Commercial Umbrella Insurance

Extra liability protection that goes above and beyond your existing business policies.

Overview

A Commercial Umbrella policy provides an extra layer of liability coverage on top of your General Liability, Commercial Auto, and Employers Liability policies. When a major claim exceeds your underlying policy limits, your umbrella coverage kicks in to protect your business assets.

What It Covers

  • Bodily injury and property damage claims that exceed underlying policy limits.
  • Personal and advertising injury liability beyond base policy limits.
  • Coverage over your General Liability, Commercial Auto, and Employers Liability policies.
  • Defense costs for covered claims (even after primary limits are exhausted).
  • High liability limits from $1 million to $10 million or more.

Why Businesses Need It

  • A single large lawsuit can exceed the limits of your base policies and threaten your business.
  • Contractors, manufacturers, and businesses with high public exposure face elevated risk.
  • Umbrella coverage is often required by clients or contracts for higher-risk projects.
  • It is one of the most cost-effective ways to significantly increase your liability protection.

Coverage Limits

  • Typically available in increments of $1 million.
  • Most small and mid-size businesses carry $1M–$5M in umbrella limits.
  • Higher limits available for larger operations or high-risk industries.
