Professional Liability (E&O)

Protection against claims of errors, omissions, or professional mistakes.

Overview

Professional Liability (Errors & Omissions) insurance protects your business from claims related to mistakes, missed deadlines, negligence, or professional services that result in financial loss for a client.

Coverage Highlights

  • Coverage for professional mistakes or oversights.
  • Protection against negligence claims.
  • Legal defense costs included.
  • Coverage for financial loss suffered by clients.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who needs Professional Liability insurance?

Professionals such as consultants, real estate agents, contractors, IT specialists, and service providers benefit from E&O coverage.

Does E&O cover intentional wrongdoing?

No. E&O covers mistakes and negligence, not intentional acts.
