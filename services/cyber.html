Cyber Liability

Protect your business from data breaches, cyberattacks, and digital threats.

Overview

Cyber Liability insurance protects your business from financial losses caused by cyberattacks, data breaches, ransomware, and other digital threats that can disrupt operations and compromise sensitive information.

Coverage Highlights

  • Data breach response and recovery.
  • Ransomware and cyber extortion coverage.
  • Business interruption due to cyber events.
  • Legal, forensic, and notification costs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Cyber Liability cover ransomware attacks?

Yes. Most cyber policies include coverage for ransomware, extortion payments, and system restoration.

Is Cyber Liability only for large companies?

No. Small and mid‑sized businesses are often the most vulnerable to cyberattacks.
