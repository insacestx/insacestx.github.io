Commercial Auto

Protect the vehicles that keep your business moving.

Overview

Commercial Auto insurance covers vehicles used for business purposes, protecting your drivers, your assets, and your operations from unexpected accidents and losses.

Coverage Highlights

  • Liability coverage for bodily injury and property damage.
  • Collision and comprehensive coverage for business vehicles.
  • Coverage for owned, hired, and non-owned vehicles.
  • Medical payments and uninsured motorist protection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need Commercial Auto if employees use their own vehicles?

Yes. You may need Non-Owned Auto coverage to protect your business from liability when employees drive their own cars for work.

Does Commercial Auto cover tools or equipment in the vehicle?

Not usually. Tools and equipment are typically covered under Inland Marine or a separate policy.
