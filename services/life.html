Life Insurance

Protect what matters most — your family's future, your home, and your legacy.

Overview

Life insurance provides financial protection for your loved ones in the event of your passing. At ACES Insurance Services, we offer a full range of life insurance products — from affordable term policies to permanent coverage with cash value, annuities for retirement income, and specialized products like Final Expense and Mortgage Protection.

Types of Life Insurance We Offer

Term Life Insurance

Provides coverage for a set period — typically 10, 20, or 30 years. It is the most affordable option and ideal for income replacement during your working years or while paying off a mortgage.

Whole Life Insurance

Permanent coverage that lasts your entire lifetime with fixed premiums and a guaranteed cash value component that grows over time. A solid choice for long-term financial planning and estate building.

Final Expense Insurance

Designed to cover funeral costs, medical bills, and other end-of-life expenses. Affordable coverage with no medical exam required in most cases, making it accessible for seniors.

Mortgage Protection Insurance

Ensures your family can continue living in your home if you pass away. The benefit is tied to your mortgage balance and pays off or reduces your home loan, giving your family stability.

IUL / Universal Life (UL)

Indexed Universal Life (IUL) and Universal Life (UL) policies offer flexible premiums and death benefits with the potential to build cash value linked to a market index (IUL) or earning a set interest rate (UL). A popular choice for retirement supplementation and tax-advantaged savings.

Annuities

Annuities are financial products that provide guaranteed income — either immediately or at a future date. They are ideal for retirement planning and ensuring you will not outlive your savings.

Life Settlement

If you have an existing life insurance policy you no longer need or can afford, a life settlement allows you to sell it to a third party for a lump sum of cash — often more than the policy's surrender value.

Benefits of Life Insurance

  • Income replacement for your family if you are the primary earner.
  • Pay off debts such as mortgages, car loans, and credit cards.
  • Fund your children's education.
  • Cover final expenses and avoid burdening your loved ones.
  • Build tax-advantaged cash value (permanent policies).
  • Supplement retirement income through annuities or policy loans.
