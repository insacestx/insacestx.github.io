General Liability

Essential protection for your business against everyday risks.

Overview

General Liability insurance protects your business from claims involving bodily injury, property damage, and personal or advertising injury. It’s one of the most important coverages for any business.

Coverage Highlights

  • Bodily injury and property damage liability.
  • Personal and advertising injury coverage.
  • Medical payments coverage.
  • Legal defense costs included.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is General Liability required for my business?

While not always legally required, most landlords, clients, and contracts require proof of General Liability coverage.

Does GL cover my employees?

No. Employee injuries are covered under Workers Compensation, not General Liability.
